About our team

We're sons, daughters, and friends

The effects of Alzheimer's on both the individual and the family are profound.

So, we are developing a ground breaking personal rescue beacon, specifically tailored for those diagnosed with Alzheimer's and Dementia, which will enable us to monitor the safety of our loved ones while giving them the opportunity to lead free and unencumbered lives.
